since it very early emergence , conception and ethical side of the advirtesement ha been harshly criticized both by the intellectual and common people . it is true that we all live in the era of globalization and capitalism of which the advirtesement constitutes ` the bread and butter ' . however , there are still significant opinion claiming for existence of certain game rule which will prevent adviretesements to make product seem much better than they really are . first , the ever-growing high competition in almost every market lead advertisement to develop such tactic . given the high density of competitor supplying , to some degree , similar product in the market , embodying some fictional quality to one of them is a lot cheaper than improving it . for instance , taking a bigger , tastier or more delightful picture of an arby 's or burger king hamburger ( most of these picture are competely fictional ) is rather easy and useful to take a better competitive position in the fast food market . it probably lead u to go and buy one of these burger and this is the point when the responsibility of advirtesements end . to solve this problem of false representation of the product in market , it is necessary to have some sort of governmental intervention and regulation . for the sake of both consumer ' and competitor firm ' right , ministry of economic affair in any given country should have the right and duty of controlling the advirtesement market accordingly . as long a we do not have such instrument , fictional product are going to stay \ on our billboard .
