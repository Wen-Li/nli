technology is ever-changing . a genius is born every now and then , who brings about new invention into the limelight . if these invention are beneficial to mankind then they soon replace the old one . one such example is the mode of transport before automobile were discovered . people either travelled by foot or they used the help of animal like horse , donkey etc . . then came the invention of the wheel after which cart were made . these cart were driven by bull and these bullock cart became the mean of travelling . further , engine were invented , which led to the production of automobile i.e. both two wheeler and four wheeler . currently many car have been manufactured and are available in the market . every year , a new model is designed , having a better engine efficiency , greater mileage , a different size or simply a different seating arrangement . these new version soon become popular in the market . just a how we have transformed from a bullock cart to a car for our mode of transport , in the same way twenty year hence there is a possibility for the invention of something else that is novel . but the question is - `` what next ? ! '' . could it be a car which fly or could it be a locomotive which look like a spaceship ? . the design of this new locomotive is left to the imagination and skill of the inventor . since , this new invention would then become famous in the market , the demand for car would eventually reduce . as a result of this , fewer car would be used twenty year hence .
