young people are the medium of carrying on the human race . yet , are they aware of their potential in creating the future ? in order to create the future , youngster need to be present in today . in other word , they have to involve actively in the problem that their society face on by helping their society . to begin with , the use of high-speed mean of technology like internet brings in the opportunity a well a the need that everything ha to be done quickly . that is to say the present era place great emphasis on individual who are sharp and quick in action . in a society where everything happens so fast , young people worry about the asset they need to have too much . therefore they do not allocate enough time and energy to the people who are out of this system like older one . in addition , one of the requirement of modern era to compete in order to get more hinders the young people to develop sensitivity to the other people who do not have chance a much a they do . as the number of people who live with inadequate resource increase , young people turn their direction away from helping poor and uneducated people . besides , today 's world are full of conflict and war . yet young people are indifferent to these issue and do not want to take part in the political decision . in this way they enable the war and conflict to go on while isolating themselves from the decision that their government make which may be harmful for their society in the long run . consequentially , because young people are too busy in meeting the need of modern age by investing themselves and competing with others , they miss the need of their society and at the same time the chance to build up a future for the successive young generation .
