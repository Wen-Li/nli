I think that most of the advertisements, if not all of them, make products seem much better than they really are.

If we watch them attentionelly , we will notice that each one claims that the cleaning product is the better to be found in the market. So if we assume that one of these cleaning products is really the best, it means that all the other advertisements are misleading the public, should we condemn them?
We should not, this kind of lie is legal, the goal of advertisement is not to mislead the public, but just encouraging people to buy a certain product.
